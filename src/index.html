About us

We are family Jovic from Petrovac na Mlavi and we are growing organic fruits and vegetables, and we further process them, on our family estate "Zukva". Property was named after the water spring of the same name, and by the fountain built by our ancestors in 1933. The word Zukva comes from the name of the grass that can be found in the fields in the environment, but it can also indicate the type of wild forest apples.

We produce our fruits and vegetables organicly, without usage of pesticides. We try to preserve autochthonous types of seeds. Some of the species we grow are: beans, tomato, onions, zucchini, peppers, cucumbers, plums... We also sell jam, brandy and ajvar. We use traditional recipes for our products.
