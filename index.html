About us

We are family Jovic from Petrovac na Mlavi and we are engaged in organic production of fruits and vegetables and their processing, on our family estate "Zukva". Property was named after the water spring of the same name, and by the fountain built by our ancestors in 1933.We produce our fruits and vegetables organicly, without usage of pesticides. Our main products are:beans, peas, tomato, onions, zucchini, peppers, cucumbers, plums... We also sell jam, brandy and ajvar.
